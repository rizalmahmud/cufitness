Cardio Workout Routine

This is your cardio workout routine. Hereâ€™s a cardio workout routine tailored to different weight classes. The intensity is adjusted based on weight and general fitness levels.

Each session: 30-45 min, 4-6 times per week

50-60kg (Lightweight)

Goal: Build endurance and cardiovascular efficiency

  • Warm-up (5 min): Jump rope, light jogging
  • Workout (30-40 min):
    1. HIIT: 30s sprint + 60s walk (repeat 10-12x)
    2. Jump Rope: 3 min rounds x 5 (30s rest in between)
    3. Burpees: 3 sets of 15
    4. Bodyweight Circuit (3 rounds):
      • Mountain Climbers (40s)
      • Jump Squats (12 reps)
      • Plank-to-Knee Drive (40s)
  • Cooldown (5 min): Stretching

60-70kg (Featherweight)

Goal: Improve endurance & agility

  • Warm-up (5 min): High knees, jumping jacks
  • Workout (35-45 min):
    1. HIIT Running: 40s sprint + 60s jog (repeat 10x)
    2. Jump Rope: 5 min rounds x 3
    3. Circuit Training (4 rounds, 30s rest each):
      • Box Jumps (12 reps)
      • Jump Lunges (10 reps/leg)
      • Burpees (12 reps)
      • Skaters (30s)
    4. Rowing Machine or Cycling (15 min steady pace or intervals)
  • Cooldown (5 min): Stretching

70-80kg (Welterweight)

Goal: Enhance cardiovascular efficiency & fat burn

  • Warm-up (5-7 min): Dynamic stretches, light jogging
  • Workout (40 min):
    1. Sprint Intervals: 45s sprint + 60s jog (repeat 8-10x)
    2. Jump Rope: 3 min rounds x 4
    3. Plyometric Circuit (4 rounds, 45s work/15s rest):
      • Explosive Push-ups
      • Box Jumps
      • High Knees
      • Jump Rope Speed Skips
    4. Stair Running (10-15 min total)
  • Cooldown (5 min): Stretching

80-90kg (Middleweight)

Goal: Fat loss, endurance, and stamina

  • Warm-up (5-7 min): Incline walking, dynamic movements
  • Workout (45 min):
    1. Treadmill HIIT: 60s sprint + 60s walk (repeat 8-10x)
    2. Jump Rope: 2 min rounds x 5
    3. Strength-Endurance Circuit (4 rounds, 30s rest each):
      • Burpees (15 reps)
      • Kettlebell Swings (12 reps)
      • Battle Ropes (40s)
      • Box Jumps (12 reps)
    4. Rowing Machine (15 min steady or interval pace)
  • Cooldown (5 min): Stretching

90-100kg & Above (Heavyweight)

Goal: Increase mobility, burn fat, improve stamina

  • Warm-up (7 min): Incline walking, jumping jacks, shoulder mobility
  • Workout (40-50 min):
    1. Treadmill Incline Walk/Run: 5 min warm-up + 10-12 min intervals (30s fast + 60s slow)
    2. Jump Rope: 1 min rounds x 6 (low impact if needed)
    3. Circuit Training (4 rounds, 45s work/15s rest):
      • Step-ups (12 reps/leg)
      • Kettlebell Swings (15 reps)
      • Medicine Ball Slams (15 reps)
      • Rowing Machine (200m sprints)
    4. Cycling or Rowing Machine (15 min steady pace)
  • Cooldown (5 min): Stretching

This plan balances fat burn, endurance, and strength across different body weights. Adjust the intensity based on your fitness level!
