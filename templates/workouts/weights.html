Weight Training Routine

This weight-training routine is based on different weight classes. The plan focuses on strength, endurance, and muscle building while aligning with individual body weights.

📝 Structure:

  • 4-5 Days per Week
  • Compound + Isolation Exercises
  • Progressive Overload (Increase weight over time)
  • Rest: 30-60 sec (Hypertrophy), 60-90 sec (Strength)

50-60kg (Lightweight)

Goal: Strength & Lean Muscle Growth

Workout Split: Full Body / Upper-Lower Split

  • Squats – 4 sets x 8-12 reps
  • Deadlifts – 3 sets x 8 reps
  • Bench Press – 3 sets x 8-12 reps
  • Pull-ups – 4 sets x 10 reps (or Assisted)
  • Overhead Press – 3 sets x 10 reps
  • Dumbbell Rows – 3 sets x 12 reps
  • Core Work: Hanging Leg Raises & Russian Twists

Key Focus:

  • Higher volume & moderate weight
  • Strength endurance
  • Keep reps between 8-12

60-70kg (Featherweight)

Goal: Strength & Definition

Workout Split: Push-Pull-Legs

  • Deadlifts – 4 sets x 6-8 reps
  • Squats – 4 sets x 8 reps
  • Bench Press – 3 sets x 8 reps
  • Pull-ups – 3 sets x 12 reps
  • Military Press – 3 sets x 10 reps
  • Dumbbell Lateral Raises – 3 sets x 12 reps
  • Face Pulls – 3 sets x 15 reps
  • Core Work: Weighted Planks

Key Focus:

  • Mix of strength and hypertrophy
  • Keep reps between 6-12
  • Heavy compound lifts

70-80kg (Welterweight)

Goal: Strength & Muscle Mass

Workout Split: Upper-Lower / Push-Pull-Legs

  • Squats – 5 sets x 6-8 reps
  • Romanian Deadlifts – 4 sets x 8 reps
  • Incline Bench Press – 4 sets x 10 reps
  • Weighted Pull-ups – 3 sets x 10 reps
  • Overhead Press – 3 sets x 8 reps
  • Barbell Rows – 3 sets x 10 reps
  • Triceps Dips – 3 sets x 12 reps
  • Core Work: Hanging Leg Raises & Cable Twists

Key Focus:

  • Moderate-to-heavy weight
  • Keep reps between 6-10
  • Increase strength & mass

80-90kg (Middleweight)

Goal: Strength & Power

Workout Split: Strength-Based (4-5 Days)

  • Deadlifts – 5 sets x 5 reps
  • Back Squats – 5 sets x 6 reps
  • Flat Bench Press – 4 sets x 8 reps
  • Bent-over Rows – 4 sets x 8 reps
  • Seated Shoulder Press – 3 sets x 10 reps
  • Farmer’s Carry – 3 sets of 30m
  • Core Work: Cable Crunches & Hanging Leg Raises

Key Focus:

  • More power-based lifts
  • Keep reps between 5-8
  • Focus on progressive overload

90-100kg & Above (Heavyweight)

Goal: Strength, Power & Fat Loss

Workout Split: Power-Based (4 Days, Heavy Weights)

  • Deadlifts – 5 sets x 4 reps
  • Front Squats – 5 sets x 5 reps
  • Bench Press – 4 sets x 6 reps
  • Weighted Dips – 4 sets x 8 reps
  • Barbell Rows – 4 sets x 8 reps
  • Farmers Walk – 4 sets of 40m
  • Sled Pushes – 3 sets x 30m
  • Core Work: Heavy Russian Twists & Cable Crunches

Key Focus:

  • Heavier lifts, fewer reps (4-6)
  • Strength & functional movement
  • Add cardio for fat loss

Final Notes

  • Progressive Overload: Increase weights over time.
  • Form First: Prioritize proper technique.
  • Recovery: Rest, hydrate, and eat well for recovery.
  • Reps & Weights:
    • 6-8 reps → Strength
    • 8-12 reps → Muscle Growth
    • 12-15 reps → Endurance
