Flexibility Workout Routine

This plan improves mobility, flexibility, and recovery—preventing injuries and enhancing performance in strength training and cardio.

📝 Structure:

  • 4-6 Days per Week
  • Hold Each Stretch for 30-60 sec
  • Repeat Each Routine 2-3x
  • Breathe Deeply & Relax

50-60kg (Lightweight)

Goal: Increase mobility & full-body flexibility

Routine: Dynamic + Static Stretches

  • Neck Tilts & Rotations – 30s each side
  • Shoulder Rolls & Cross-Body Stretch – 30s each side
  • Cat-Cow Stretch – 10 reps
  • Downward Dog to Cobra Stretch – 5 reps (hold each for 10s)
  • Standing Forward Fold – 45s
  • Seated Hamstring Stretch – 30s each leg
  • Butterfly Stretch – 40s
  • Spinal Twist (Seated or Lying) – 30s per side

Key Focus:

  • Increase overall mobility
  • Improve posture & recovery

60-70kg (Featherweight)

Goal: Improve muscle elasticity & prevent tightness

Routine: Pre/Post-Workout Focus

  • Arm Circles & Shoulder Stretch – 30s each
  • Chest Opener Stretch (Doorway Stretch) – 40s
  • Cat-Cow & Child’s Pose – 10 reps each
  • Standing Quad Stretch – 30s per leg
  • Seated Forward Fold (Pike Stretch) – 45s
  • Lunge with Side Bend (Hip Flexor Stretch) – 30s per side
  • Frog Stretch – 45s
  • Seated Spinal Twist – 30s per side

Key Focus:

  • Improve hip & shoulder mobility
  • Reduce tightness from workouts

70-80kg (Welterweight)

Goal: Improve flexibility while maintaining strength

Routine: Post-Workout / Active Recovery Days

  • Wall Shoulder Stretch – 30s
  • Chest Stretch on the Floor – 30s per side
  • Cat-Cow & Thread the Needle – 8 reps
  • Seated Hip Flexor Stretch – 30s per side
  • Frog Pose (Deep Groin Stretch) – 45s
  • Hamstring Stretch (One-Leg Elevated on Bench) – 30s per side
  • Glute Stretch (Figure-4 Stretch) – 30s per side
  • Supine Twist – 30s per side

Key Focus:

  • Improve joint mobility
  • Loosen up tight hamstrings & hips

80-90kg (Middleweight)

Goal: Prevent stiffness & enhance range of motion

Routine: Mobility & Static Stretching Mix

  • Neck & Shoulder Rolls – 30s
  • Overhead Lat Stretch – 30s per side
  • Spiderman Lunge with Reach – 5 reps per side
  • Seated Forward Fold – 45s
  • Pigeon Pose (Glute Stretch) – 30s per side
  • Butterfly Stretch (Hip Opener) – 40s
  • Standing Side Bend Stretch – 30s per side
  • Cobra Stretch & Child’s Pose – 45s each

Key Focus:

  • Prevent tightness from strength training
  • Improve lower body flexibility

90-100kg & Above (Heavyweight)

Goal: Increase flexibility & relieve joint pressure

Routine: Focus on Recovery & Deep Stretches

  • Neck & Shoulder Stretch – 30s each
  • Wall Chest Stretch – 30s per side
  • Cat-Cow & Thread the Needle – 5 reps per side
  • Seated Hamstring Stretch – 45s
  • Lunging Hip Flexor Stretch – 30s per side
  • Pigeon Pose (Glute & Hip Stretch) – 30s per side
  • Wide-Leg Forward Fold – 45s
  • Reclining Spinal Twist – 30s per side

Key Focus:

  • Reduce stiffness & improve joint mobility
  • Prevent injury from weight-bearing workouts

Final Notes

  • ✔ Stretch Daily – Even 10-15 min helps
  • ✔ Use Foam Rolling – Especially for legs, back, and shoulders
  • ✔ Combine Dynamic (Pre-Workout) & Static (Post-Workout) Stretching
  • ✔ Listen to Your Body – Avoid overstretching
