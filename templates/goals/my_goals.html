Diet Plan for 2 kg Weight Loss

  • Breakfast: Oatmeal with berries and a small handful of almonds
  • Snack: Greek yogurt with honey
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens and vinaigrette
  • Snack: A piece of fruit (apple or orange)
  • Dinner: Steamed fish, quinoa, and steamed vegetables
  • Hydration: At least 8 glasses of water
  • Recommended Daily Calorie Intake: 2100 calories
Note: This plan is ideal for 80kg body weight people.

Diet Plan for 5 kg Weight Loss

  • Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with spinach and whole-grain toast
  • Snack: A small smoothie (banana, spinach, protein powder, water)
  • Lunch: Turkey wrap with whole wheat tortilla and lots of veggies
  • Snack: Carrot sticks with hummus
  • Dinner: Baked salmon, brown rice, and broccoli
  • Hydration: 8-10 glasses of water daily
  • Recommended Daily Calorie Intake: 1900 calories
Note: This plan is ideal for 80kg body weight people.

Diet Plan for 7 kg Weight Loss

  • Breakfast: Smoothie bowl with mixed berries, banana, and a sprinkle of granola
  • Snack: Cottage cheese with sliced pineapple
  • Lunch: Quinoa salad with black beans, corn, diced tomatoes, and avocado
  • Snack: A handful of mixed nuts
  • Dinner: Grilled lean beef or tofu, sweet potato, and green beans
  • Hydration: At least 10 glasses of water daily
  • Recommended Daily Calorie Intake: 1700 calories
Note: This plan is ideal for 80kg body weight people.

Diet Plan for 10 kg Weight Loss

  • Breakfast: Egg white omelette with vegetables and a side of fresh fruit
  • Snack: Protein shake with water and a piece of fruit
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken or tempeh with mixed salad greens, quinoa, and a light dressing
  • Snack: Sliced cucumber and bell peppers with a light dip
  • Dinner: Baked cod or lentil stew with a variety of steamed vegetables
  • Hydration: At least 10-12 glasses of water daily
  • Recommended Daily Calorie Intake: 1500 calories
Note: This plan is ideal for 80kg body weight people.
